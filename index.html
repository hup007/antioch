News Update

Guest Information

In line with the latest Government guidance, and following confirmation that Singapore is moving into Phase 3 of re-opening, our Park and Hotels are now open. We’re looking forward to seeing your visits and, as always, your safety and our team’s safety remains our number one priority. Like before, we have additional hygiene and safety measures in place across the whole Resort, including temperature checks on arrival, enhanced cleaning, one-way systems, and social distancing markers in our ride queues, shops, restaurants and more.

We ask guests to respect the Government guidance and not socialise with anybody outside of your household or social bubble, and SafeEntry check-ins will be compulsory upon each guest's arrival. Let’s all stay united and disciplined in our fight towards COVID-19, while enjoying the facilities of our park.
