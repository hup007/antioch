The Rise of Herobrine

Herobrine® Valley

Venture into the unknown side of Minecraft and discover the haunting story of its mythical antagonist, Herobrine. Go on an ferris wheel adventure to explore the darkness in attempt to hunt down the mythical Herobrine, but be careful, else the hunter becomes hunted...

Phasmophobia®: The Awakening

Phasmophobia® Valley

Ages 16 and up

From the creators of Phasmophobia, experience a specially made spinoff. Venture through an abandoned prison, rumored to be haunted by the ghosts of yore... Equip yourself with an arsenal of ghost-busting tools, and set out to collect as much information as possible about the prison's haunting inhabitants. Tread carefully and think smart, for the outcome of the expedition depends on you.

Phasmophobia® Virtual Chamber

Phasmophobia® Valley

Ages 16 and up

Step into a virtual world unlike any you've seen before. Our cutting edge VR technology is not only capable of complete 360° rotation both vertically and horizontally, but also comes equipped with a full 30m drop chute for the full heart-pounding experience! Choose from our carefully curated selection of games, ranging from shooters to parkour, and get ready for an unforgettable experience.

Among Us: Silent Danger

Impostor Path

1m min. height

Trapped on a spaceship with one goal— Return to earth. Grab all your friends and family for fantastic fun on our classic Among Us bumper car ride. Prepare to outrun your favourite frenemy and take the chance on getting spun out.

A Walk of Lights

Twesta Idea

Looking for quieter areas during peak periods? Head over to Twesta Idea and check out our custom-made light show. En-route to the spectacular 370m long walk, visitors can take 20 to 30mins to complete a walk around the lightshow.

A ride to Herobrine

Herobrine® Valley

You may get wet

Take high-speed water slides and uncover this secret waterpark of Herobrine! Standing nearly 60 feet tall and over 400 feet of slide, the new mat racing water slide will feature 6 side-by-side racing lanes for you and your friends. Be quick, or he'll sneak up on you before you know it!

Twesta Log Flume

Twesta Idea

1m min. height

You may get wet

An industry classic, our ride offers a nice, relaxing, and dry ride... right up until that drop at the end that is. As one of our original attractions, Twesta Log Flume ride has been a family favourite for years. Not only is it a blast for the riders, it is also fun for the onlookers waiting to soak the incoming log with our hidden water cannons.

Splash Battle: Imposter's Reign

Impostor Path

You may get wet

Take a cruise on our 250ft. long waterway in a water spaceship splash-blast for the whole family. Explore our custom-made spaceship and complete all water activities to win. Complete all the tasks or expose the impostor to win— or if you're the imposter, take out the crew and stop them from returning to earth. A brand-new fun-for-all with a unique twist!

Location

Shuttle bus from / to Saint Mario MRT Station every 30 minutes from 8am to 10pm on Tuesdays to Sundays.

Alternatively, buses 21, 39, 12 and 59 can be taken from Saint Mario MRT Station to 200m from the park entrance.

There are total of 5,000 parking spaces. The hourly parking rates is $2.50 per hour. Full-day parkings are also available. (Please enquire at the customer counter at park lobby.)
